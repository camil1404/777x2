GE9X

The GE9X is the largest and most powerful commercial aircraft engine ever built, incorporating advanced technologies that enable more efficient, quieter and with fewer emissions. Designed specifically for the new twin-engine Boeing 777X family, the GE9X is the most fuel-efficient engine in its class, delivering unmatched reliability and performance.

EFFICIENCY

Fewer fan blades provide greater efficiency. Reduced fan blade thickness improves aerodynamic efficiency, while its lower fan radius ratio maximizes air flow and minimizes drag. Combined with fewer fan blades than its commercial counterparts, GE9X boasts the most efficient fan to increase performance and decrease fuel burn.

GE90 - 22 blades
